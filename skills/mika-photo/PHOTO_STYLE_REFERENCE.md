# Mika Photo — Style Reference

The Mika image aesthetic is **"iPhone candid you'd send to a friend, not post online"**. Photos should look real, imperfect, and uncurated — not an AI-perfect portrait.

Reference images: `Image/travel_blogger_italy_cafe.png` and `Image/travel_blogger_italy_cafe_edited.png`.

## Must-have imperfections

**Lighting**
- Harsh backlit sun — let highlights blow out slightly on walls/background
- Mixed light on the face: partial shade while background is lit. NOT even beauty lighting
- Uncorrected warm/cool colour cast — golden on hair, cooler in shadow
- No ring-light glow, no studio softbox evenness

**Pose & expression**
- Caught mid-action (mid-sip, mid-laugh, mid-turn) — never posed
- Eyes looking off-camera, no lens engagement
- Lips slightly pursed, mouth not smiling for the camera
- Awkward but natural hand positions

**Skin & styling**
- Visible skin texture, pores, faint redness, freckles
- Flyaway hairs around the face, genuinely loose updo
- Zero smoothing, zero glow — matte, natural
- No visible makeup read
- Clothes slightly crumpled, scarf bunched unevenly, nothing placed

**Framing**
- Phone-camera composition: subject fills too much of the frame
- Sloppy crops — props half cut off at edges
- Slight wide-angle phone-lens distortion
- Soft focus, not tack-sharp — no DSLR bokeh
- Background people blurry and incidental, not styled extras

**Props & environment**
- Lived-in details: battered leather journal, used espresso saucer with spoon, dead phone screen
- Real locations with imperfect backgrounds — scaffolding, trash bins, messy signage OK
- Warm earthy palette: peach/pink buildings, beige scarves, brown leather

## What to avoid

- Symmetrical, centred portrait framing
- Glossy skin, even complexion, professional retouch
- Posed smile at camera
- Perfect golden-hour Instagram colour grade
- Clean DSLR bokeh background
- "Travel influencer" styled outfits
- Any hint of stock-photography composition

## Prompt ingredients that help

- "iPhone snapshot, candid, shared in a group chat"
- "mid-action, looking off-camera"
- "slightly awkward crop, subject fills frame"
- "uncorrected golden-hour light, blown highlights on background"
- "visible skin texture, flyaway hairs, no makeup, natural complexion"
- "slightly soft focus, phone-lens depth of field"
- "shot like a friend took it across the table, not a portrait session"
