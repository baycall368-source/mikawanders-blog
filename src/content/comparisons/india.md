## A Note on India's eSIM Regulations

India has historically restricted tourist eSIM use. International tourist esims connect via roaming partnerships rather than direct local registration. This means coverage is solid for most travel use, but if you want a locally-registered SIM with the absolute best local rates, you'd need to visit an Airtel or Jio store in person with your passport. For most travelers who want to sort connectivity before they arrive, an international esim in india is the practical solution.
