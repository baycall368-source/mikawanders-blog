## How to Choose the Right eSIM for Austria

**How long is your trip?** A long weekend in Vienna means 1-2GB is plenty with hotel Wi-Fi at night. A week taking in Vienna, Salzburg, and the Salzkammergut lake district? 3-5GB handles it comfortably. A ski season or extended Alpine stay with remote work? The 10gb plan at $9.95 is a genuine bargain, one of the cheapest in all of Europe. The amount of mobile data you need depends on your usage, but most travelers do well with 3-5GB.

**Do you need unlimited data?** Probably not for a standard Austria trip. Vienna has excellent cafe Wi-Fi and a reliable internet connection in most accommodations. Most travelers use 3-5GB over two weeks. If you're doing video calls from a mountain lodge, Nomad's unlimited data plans exist for that. But eSIMply's fixed plans are the better starting point for most people, and much better value for money.

**Are you staying in Austria or moving through central Europe?** Austria is often a stop on a broader trip through Germany, Switzerland, Hungary, Czech Republic, France, or Italy. If that's your situation, regional plans from Airalo or Roamless may simplify things across borders. For Austria only, eSIMply's country-specific pricing is hard to beat. The best esim for Europe overall depends on how many countries you're visiting.

**What about Alpine coverage?** In resort towns, coverage is generally fine on A1's mobile network. On popular hiking trails and ski slopes, signal holds up reasonably well. In remote backcountry terrain, don't rely on an internet connection. Download offline Google Maps and mountain route data before you head out. This travel tip applies regardless of which esim provider you choose.

**Do you need a local Austrian phone number?** All five esim providers here are data-only. For WhatsApp, Google Maps, Facebook, and browsing, you don't need a local number. If you specifically need to receive text messages or make voice calls and SMS on an Austrian number, Holafly includes phone number functionality on some plans. Most travelers stay connected just fine with data-only.

**Can I tether my laptop?** Yes, all the prepaid esim data plans above support tethering. Useful if you're working from a laptop at an Alpine lodge or Vienna cafe. Tethering burns through mobile data faster than phone use alone, so account for that when choosing your package size.
