## How to Choose the Right eSIM for Poland

**How long are you going?** A long weekend in Kraków means 1-2GB is probably enough with WiFi at your accommodation. Two weeks touring the country including Warsaw, Kraków, Wrocław, and Gdańsk? Go with 5GB at minimum. A month-long stay with remote work? The 10GB plan handles it comfortably.

**Do you actually need unlimited data?** Most travelers to Poland don't. The country isn't particularly data-heavy to navigate. Mobile data for maps, messaging, some browsing. I've used under 4GBs on active two-week trips here. Unlimited data in Poland costs significantly more than fixed plans. Unless you're streaming Netflix all day or video calling multiple times daily, a fixed prepaid esim is better value. Fair usage policies apply to unlimited tiers regardless of which provider you choose.

**Staying in Poland or covering multiple countries?** Poland sits between Germany, Czech Republic, and Ukraine, making it a natural stop on longer Eastern European itineraries. Whether you're traveling to Warsaw only or doing a regional circuit, Airalo's regional plans or Roamless's flexible data balance start making more sense than a Poland-only esim if you're crossing into multiple countries. Either way, an eSIM eliminates international roaming charges entirely. For Poland alone, eSIMply's prepaid esim for poland is the clear pick.

**Do you need a local telephone number?** All five providers above are data-only. WhatsApp, Telegram, and Google Maps work perfectly on internet access. If you need a local Polish telephone number for something like SMS confirmation, you'll need Holafly or a physical SIM card from a local physical store. Most travelers don't need this.

**Can I use my esim across multiple devices?** Your esim lives on your mobile phone's esim chip. You can share internet access without a separate plan via hotspot on multiple devices, which works fine for occasional laptop use. All providers here support this. Hotspot use burns through your GBs faster, so account for that in your plan choice.
