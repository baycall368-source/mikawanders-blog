**Are you doing the Gulf circuit?** You're in the UAE but continuing to Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, or Qatar. Airalo's regional esims for the united arab and surrounding countries, or Roamless's multi-country balance, start making more financial sense than buying individual esims for united arab emirates and each other country separately.

**What about the VoIP restriction?** VoIP apps like WhatsApp calling, FaceTime, and Skype voice calls are blocked on UAE networks. This applies to ALL esims for uae. It's a UAE regulatory thing, not a provider limitation. Text messaging on all these apps works fine. Plan your communication setup around this. **Do you need a local number?** The UAE are data-only esims across the board. No local UAE phone number included. For most travel purposes this is fine. If you need to receive local SMS for banking or booking platforms, a physical sim card from the airport kiosk covers that while your esim handles data.
